Assistant Director of SBI Gaming.

The Assistant Director of SBI Gaming is a student held volunteer (or work study) part-time position. Duties of this position will involve:

(2) help plan, promote, and run the annual Esport Olympics gaming tournament at UBCon.
(3) aid the Director of SBI gaming with anything he/she may need. The applicant is expected to be knowledgeable about Esports and popular competitive games (League of Legends, Hearthstone, etc.).
