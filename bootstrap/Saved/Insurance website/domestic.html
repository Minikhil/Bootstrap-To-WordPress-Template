All qualifying full-time domestic and undergraduate students have a mandatory medical insurance requirement. (Graduate 9+ credit hours-undergratuate 12+ credit hours) Veterans and part-time students should contact the Student Medical Insurance office for more information if required.


Sub Board One, Inc., at the University at Buffalo contracts with Gallagher Student Health for the Medical Insurance waiver process. All waiver Questions and Concerns should be directed to service at gallagherstudent.com/buffalo or by phone toll free 877-231-9757

REQUIREMENTS FOR THE MEDICAL INSURANCE WAIVER APPLICATION.

Private Insurance must coverage must be in effect and include:

  • Physician Visits in the Buffalo, NY area
  • Lab and X-rays.
  • Hospital Procedures and Visits.
  • Prescription Drugs

Off Cycle Enrollment application will require a certificate of prior private coverage termincation date. Forms can be scanned to asksmi@buffalo.edu
