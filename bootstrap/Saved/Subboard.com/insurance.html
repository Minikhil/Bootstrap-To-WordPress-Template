Student Insurance Plans

Domestic Students
Sub Board I, Inc. has negotiated a plan with lower premium costs and lower co-pays for visits with Blue Cross and Blue Shield of WNY for the domestic students. Plan benefits are effective August 22 through August 21 of the following year. For details about the plan, links to find a participating provider, or to contact Blue Cross and Blue Shield of WNY about benefits, click here.
All domestic waiver questions and concerns must be directed to: www.gallagherstudent.com customer service at :
(877) 231-9757

International Students/Scholars/OPT
International students, Scholars, and OPT students have a global insurance policy available to them, contracted by SUNY through GeoBlue. To search for a provider, get detailed plan benefits or to contact GeoBlue directly, click here. Students and scholars who are adequately insured may be able to waive the GeoBlue insurance plan by providing proof of comparable coverage. Restrictions and deadlines apply. Click here to access the wavier packet.
